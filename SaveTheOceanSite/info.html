Water Pollution | Why Care?




If water pollution is not fixed within the next few decades,
clean water may become as rare as gold.

In 2019, the WHO noted that 785 million people lacked access to drinking water.
This led to 120,000 cholera-related deaths that year.

Water pollution has the greatest impact on the life that depends on it, which is everyone.
Contamination of water can impact hundreds, even thousands of oceanic life,
whether they are marine, airborne, or flightless.
